My approach to interview tutoring

I am an experienced medical school interview tutor, with a strong interest in helping applicants identify and showcase their genuine values and strengths during the application process. I have been lucky to have the opportunity to refine my interview skills over the past 10 years both by preparing for and obtaining competitive grants, scholarships and positions and by assisting others to do the same. To my mind the key to performing well in medical school interviews is developing an ability to genuinely known your own values, to have the flexibility of thought to consider the perspectives of others, and to apply structured and nuanced frameworks for responding to common question types. I typically run all my sessions as mock interviews with detailed feedback provided. I believe the best way to get good at interviews is by doing interview practice.
